Sage 100 (MAS 90/MAS 200) to QuickBooks Conversion Experts

Professional ERP extraction and accounting migration services

If your business has outgrown basic accounting software, you might be running Sage 100—formerly known as MAS 90 or MAS 200. While it’s a highly capable ERP system that handles manufacturing, distribution, and complex inventory, it comes with a heavy price tag. Between the SQL server maintenance, expensive IT consultants required for basic fixes, and rigid user interface, many mid-sized businesses find themselves looking for an exit. Migrating from Sage 100 to QuickBooks Enterprise or QuickBooks Online is a strategic move to drastically reduce IT overhead without losing advanced functionality.

Extracting data from a modular ERP isn't easy. Sage 100 stores data in separate modules (General Ledger, Accounts Payable, Sales Order, etc.) inside a complex SQL database. You can't just export a CSV file. Our accounting data conversion experts bypass the user interface and extract data directly from the SQL backend tables. We flatten those complex relationships and map your historical GL detail, customer profiles, open orders, and inventory assemblies into QuickBooks' native structure.

One of the biggest fears businesses have during an ERP to accounting software conversion is losing historical General Ledger detail. We eliminate this risk by running a pre-migration assessment and a strict post-migration reconciliation. We compare your Profit and Loss (P&L) and Balance Sheet reports in Sage 100 against your newly migrated QuickBooks file down to the penny. This verification process guarantees that your financial history, account balances, and reporting continuity are preserved with 100% accuracy.

For manufacturing and wholesale distribution companies, inventory management is critical. Sage 100’s Inventory Requirements planning (IRP) and Bill of Materials modules are powerful, but QuickBooks Enterprise Advanced Inventory offers a more modern, user-friendly approach. We map your Sage 100 assemblies, quantities on hand, and average costs directly into QuickBooks Enterprise, enabling features like barcode scanning, multi-warehouse tracking, and FIFO costing without the clunky ERP interface.

Why abandon Sage 100 (MAS 90/200)? Legacy ERP systems require constant server maintenance, version upgrades, and expensive third-party consultants just to pull custom reports. By moving to QuickBooks, your business gains access to automated bank feeds, 700+ native app integrations, and a simplified user interface that your entire team can actually navigate with confidence.

We also handle the migration of your open Sales Orders and Purchase Orders. We know that your fulfillment pipeline can't stop just because you are changing software. By bringing over your open transactions, your warehouse and sales teams can pick up right where they left off in QuickBooks, ensuring zero disruption to your daily operations.

Don't let ERP bloat and expensive IT maintenance slow down your business growth. Trust CountRights to securely handle your Sage 100 to QuickBooks data migration. We bring the expertise, the SQL extraction tools, and the accounting knowledge required to ensure a seamless, zero-data-loss transition.
