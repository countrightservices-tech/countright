Frequently Asked Questions

Common questions about bookkeeping and our resources.

What is bookkeeping?

Bookkeeping is the process of recording, organizing and maintaining a business's financial transactions to ensure accurate financial records.

Why is bookkeeping important?

Accurate bookkeeping helps businesses monitor cash flow, prepare for taxes, make informed decisions and stay financially organized.

Do small businesses need bookkeeping?

Yes. Every business, regardless of size, benefits from organized financial records, accurate reporting and better cash flow management.

Can Countrights help remotely?

Yes. Countrights provides remote bookkeeping and accounting support for businesses using modern cloud accounting software.
