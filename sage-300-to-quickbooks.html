Sage 300 (Accpac) to QuickBooks Conversion Experts

Professional multi-currency ERP extraction and international accounting migration

Sage 300—formerly known as Accpac—is a true enterprise-grade ERP built specifically for mid-sized companies handling complex international operations. While it excels at multi-currency tracking and multi-entity consolidations, it comes with a heavy burden. Maintaining a Sage 300 environment requires dedicated IT staff, expensive SQL server hosting, and constant version upgrades. For many growing businesses, migrating from Sage 300 to QuickBooks Enterprise or QuickBooks Online is a strategic move to drastically reduce IT overhead while maintaining the financial visibility they need.

Multi-currency data extraction is not for the faint of heart. Sage 300 stores data in a complex SQL structure, separating home currency amounts from foreign currency amounts. You can't just export a spreadsheet. Our accounting data conversion experts bypass the user interface and extract data directly from the SQL backend tables. We flatten those complex relationships and map your historical GL detail, foreign exchange rates, and international customer profiles into QuickBooks' multi-currency structure.

One of the biggest fears businesses have during an international ERP conversion is losing the accuracy of their foreign exchange balances. We eliminate this risk by running a pre-migration assessment and a strict post-migration reconciliation. We compare your Profit and Loss (P&L) and Balance Sheet reports in Sage 300 against your newly migrated QuickBooks file down to the penny. This verification process guarantees that your unrealized gains/losses, account balances, and reporting continuity are preserved with 100% accuracy.

For companies operating multiple legal entities, Sage 300's multi-company structure can be a blessing and a curse. If you no longer need separate databases, we can consolidate multiple Sage 300 company ledgers into a single QuickBooks Enterprise file. We use 'Classes' or 'Locations' to track the different branches, giving you consolidated reporting at the click of a button without the headache of managing multiple SQL databases.

Why abandon Sage 300 (Accpac)? Legacy ERP systems require constant server maintenance, expensive IT consultants, and clunky remote desktop setups just to access your data. By moving to QuickBooks, your business gains access to automated bank feeds, 700+ native app integrations, and a simplified user interface that your entire international team can actually navigate with confidence.

We also handle the migration of your open Accounts Receivable and Accounts Payable invoices in foreign currencies. We ensure that the foreign currency amounts and their home currency equivalents are perfectly mapped so your vendor and customer balances are accurate from day one in QuickBooks.

Don't let ERP bloat and expensive international IT maintenance slow down your business growth. Trust CountRights to securely handle your Sage 300 to QuickBooks data migration. We bring the expertise, the SQL extraction tools, and the multi-currency accounting knowledge required to ensure a seamless, zero-data-loss transition.
