Sage Intacct to QuickBooks Conversion Experts

Professional cloud ERP extraction and dimension mapping services

Sage Intacct is widely recognized as a top-tier cloud financial management system. It’s powerful, highly customizable, and great for non-profits and multi-entity corporations. However, many mid-sized businesses realize too late that they are paying a premium price for enterprise features they simply don't use. If your team is only using Intacct for basic AP, AR, and financial reporting, migrating from Sage Intacct to QuickBooks Online or QuickBooks Enterprise is a strategic move to drastically reduce your monthly SaaS overhead without losing the financial visibility you need.

Mapping Intacct Dimensions requires expert handling. Sage Intacct relies on "Dimensions" (like Department, Location, Project, or Custom Fields) to tag transactions. QuickBooks handles this differently, using Classes, Locations, and Categories. Our accounting data conversion experts meticulously map your Intacct dimensions directly into QuickBooks' tracking structure, ensuring your granular departmental reporting remains perfectly intact and accurate year-over-year.

One of the biggest fears businesses have during a cloud ERP conversion is losing historical General Ledger detail. We eliminate this risk by running a pre-migration assessment and a strict post-migration reconciliation. We compare your Profit and Loss (P&L) and Balance Sheet reports in Sage Intacct against your newly migrated QuickBooks file down to the penny. This verification process guarantees that your financial history, account balances, and reporting continuity are preserved with 100% accuracy.

For companies operating multiple legal entities, Sage Intacct’s multi-entity consolidation is a powerful feature. However, if you no longer need separate legal entities, we can consolidate multiple Intacct company databases into a single QuickBooks Enterprise file. We use 'Classes' or 'Locations' to track the different branches, giving you consolidated reporting at the click of a button without the headache of paying for multiple Intacct entity licenses.

Why abandon Sage Intacct? Intacct is a premium SaaS platform with high per-user licensing costs, complex implementation requirements, and a steep learning curve. By moving to QuickBooks, your business gains access to native US payroll, automated bank feeds, 700+ app integrations, and a simplified user interface that your entire team can actually navigate with confidence—all while cutting software costs by up to 60%.

We also handle the migration of your open Accounts Receivable and Accounts Payable invoices. We ensure that all outstanding customer and vendor balances are perfectly mapped so your aging reports are accurate from day one in QuickBooks. Your fulfillment pipeline won't skip a beat, and your vendors will be paid on time.

Don't let expensive SaaS bloat and complex ERP features slow down your business growth. Trust CountRights to securely handle your Sage Intacct to QuickBooks data migration. We bring the expertise, the API extraction tools, and the accounting knowledge required to ensure a seamless, zero-data-loss transition.
