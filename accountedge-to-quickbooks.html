AccountEdge to QuickBooks Conversion Experts

Professional accounting migration services for businesses

As businesses grow, many find that desktop-based accounting software like AccountEdge limits their ability to scale, collaborate remotely, or integrate with modern cloud applications. CountRights provides professional AccountEdge to QuickBooks migration services designed to transition your business to the cloud seamlessly. Whether you are moving to QuickBooks Online for remote accessibility or QuickBooks Desktop for advanced inventory management, our team ensures a zero-data-loss conversion.

Migrating from AccountEdge requires more than just exporting a spreadsheet. It involves complex data mapping, specifically when translating AccountEdge’s unique chart of accounts structures, historical journal entries, and inventory assemblies into QuickBooks' native format. Our accounting data conversion experts meticulously map your chart of accounts, customer and vendor lists, open invoices, unpaid bills, and historical financial transactions to ensure that your comparative reports remain perfectly accurate year-over-year.

One of the biggest challenges businesses face during an accounting software conversion is mismatched opening balances. We eliminate this issue by running a pre-migration assessment and a post-migration reconciliation. We compare your Profit and Loss (P&L) and Balance Sheet reports in AccountEdge against your newly migrated QuickBooks file. This strict verification process guarantees that your financial history, account balances, and reporting continuity are preserved with 100% accuracy.

By upgrading to QuickBooks, your business gains access to advanced financial reporting, automated bank feeds, and a massive ecosystem of third-party integrations. Don't let messy data or a complicated software transition slow down your business growth. Trust CountRights to securely handle your AccountEdge to QuickBooks data migration so you can focus on running your business with clear, reliable financial data.
