Sage 50 (Peachtree) to QuickBooks Conversion Experts

Professional desktop to cloud accounting migration services

Sage 50—formerly known to many as Peachtree Accounting—is a reliable desktop software that has served small businesses for decades. But if you're reading this, you've probably hit the limits of what a desktop-based system can do for a growing company. Between the manual backups, the hassle of setting up remote desktop access for your CPA, and the clunky integrations, it's easy to see why so many businesses are looking to migrate from Sage 50 to QuickBooks.

It's not just about exporting a CSV file. Converting Peachtree to QuickBooks requires careful data mapping. The way Sage 50 handles its chart of accounts, inventory assemblies, and historical journal entries is structurally different from QuickBooks. Our accounting data conversion experts meticulously map your customer profiles, outstanding invoices, vendor bills, and historical financial transactions to ensure that your comparative reports remain perfectly accurate year-over-year.

One of the biggest headaches businesses face during an accounting software conversion is mismatched opening balances. We eliminate this issue by running a pre-migration assessment and a post-migration reconciliation. We compare your Profit and Loss (P&L) and Balance Sheet reports in Sage 50 against your newly migrated QuickBooks file. This strict verification process guarantees that your financial history and account balances are preserved with 100% accuracy.

For businesses that have been using Sage 50 for years, you might be worried about losing your historical data. Don't be. We can migrate years of past financial data, keeping your comparative reporting intact so you can look back at previous years' performance without missing a beat. Whether you are moving to QuickBooks Online for team collaboration or QuickBooks Desktop for advanced inventory management, our team ensures a zero-data-loss conversion.

Why abandon legacy Sage desktop software? Desktop systems require manual backups, remote desktop setups for outside CPAs, and lack real-time bank feed integrations. By moving to QuickBooks Online, your business gains the flexibility of cloud-based remote access, automated daily bank syncing, and seamless multi-user collaboration without the IT overhead.

By transitioning to QuickBooks, your business gains access to advanced financial reporting, automated bank feeds, and a massive ecosystem of over 700 third-party integrations.
